Privacy Policy

Instatools is designed to keep your work local. This page explains how the tools handle your content and what (if any) third-party resources are used.

Local-only image processing

During design and export, your images are processed in your browser. Instatools does not upload your images or design data to a server as part of creating posts, covers, watermarks, or previews.

No account required (for core use)

Instatools is a browser-based design suite. In most cases, you can use the tools without creating an account.

Third-party static assets

The marketing pages and UI may load static resources (like fonts and CSS) from external providers to render the interface. This does not change how your images are processed during the design workflow.

Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Check this page for the latest version.

This is not legal advice. If you have questions, consider reaching out via the project’s GitHub page.
